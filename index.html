Budget Tracker Command-Line Tool

A simple command-line tool that allows users to input their financial items and allocate money to create a budget they can stick to each month.

What is Budget Tracker?

The Budget Tracker CLI helps users to organize and manage their finances efficiently. With this tool, you can:

  • Input various expense categories
  • Allocate specific amounts of money to each category
  • Track your spending and adjust your budget accordingly

It is a simple, easy-to-use tool to help you stick to your budget and make smarter financial decisions.
